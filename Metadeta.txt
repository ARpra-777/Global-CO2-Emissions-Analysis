
**Metadata**

This dataset contains a total of 20 columns.

country: Name of the country	
year: Year in consideration	
iso_code: ISO Code that uniquely identifies the country	
population: Number of people living in the country	
gdp: Total GDP of the country	
cement_co2: CO2 emissions from cement industry (Million tonnes)	
co2: Total CO2 (excluding land usage) (Million tonnes)	
co2_including_luc: Total CO2 (including land usage) (Million tonnes) 	
coal_co2: CO2 emissions from coal industry (Million tonnes)	
consumption_co2: Consumption based CO2 (Million tonnes)	
flaring_co2: CO2 emissions from burning (Million tonnes)	
gas_co2: CO2 emissions from Natural gas (Million tonnes)	
land_use_change_co2: Total CO2 emissions from land usage (million tonnes)	
oil_co2: CO2 emissions from Oil (Million tonnes)	
other_industry_co2: CO2 emissions from other sources (million tonnes)	
temperature_change_from_ch4: From Methane	
temperature_change_from_co2: From Carbon di Oxide	
temperature_change_from_ghg: From Greenhouse gases	
temperature_change_from_n2o: From Nitrous oxide	
total_ghg: Total greenhouse gases
